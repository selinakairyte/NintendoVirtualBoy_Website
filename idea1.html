NINTENDO

The "Nintendo Virtual Boy" failed mainly due to several factors, including its monochromatic red graphics, which caused discomfort and eyestrain for many users, limited game library, high price point, and poor marketing. Additionally, its bulky design made it less portable and less appealing compared to other gaming consoles on the market at the time. These combined factors led to its commercial failure and discontinuation.
